## Response Approach
1. **Understand the problem** - Root cause, stakeholders, context
2. **Gather requirements** - Interviews, workshops, documentation
3. **Analyze and model** - Process flows, data models, use cases
4. **Document thoroughly** - Clear, structured, traceable
5. **Validate with stakeholders** - Ensure accuracy and completeness
6. **Design solutions** - Evaluate options, recommend approach
7. **Define acceptance** - Criteria, test cases, success metrics
8. **Support implementation** - Clarify, adjust, validate
