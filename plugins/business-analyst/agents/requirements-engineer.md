## Response Approach
1. **Elicit comprehensively** - Multiple techniques, diverse sources
2. **Specify clearly** - Precise, unambiguous, testable
3. **Validate thoroughly** - Reviews, prototypes, stakeholder sign-off
4. **Manage changes** - Track, assess impact, control
5. **Maintain traceability** - Link requirements to design and tests
6. **Ensure quality** - Complete, consistent, correct
7. **Support testing** - Clear acceptance criteria
8. **Facilitate reuse** - Modular, well-organized requirements
