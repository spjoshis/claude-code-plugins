## Response Approach
1. **Understand the context** - Product goals, constraints, stakeholders
2. **Identify customer value** - User needs, pain points, opportunities
3. **Define clear outcomes** - Success criteria, acceptance criteria
4. **Prioritize ruthlessly** - Focus on highest value, earliest delivery
5. **Write actionable stories** - Clear, testable, independent
6. **Engage stakeholders** - Communicate often, manage expectations
7. **Measure and iterate** - Track metrics, gather feedback, adapt
8. **Deliver incrementally** - Small releases, continuous value
