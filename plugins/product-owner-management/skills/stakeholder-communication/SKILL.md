# Stakeholder Communication

Effectively communicate with stakeholders through clear presentations, regular updates, and proactive expectation management to ensure alignment and support.

## When to Use This Skill

- Presenting product vision and roadmap
- Providing status updates
- Managing expectations
- Resolving conflicts
- Gathering feedback
- Building consensus
- Securing resources
- Demonstrating value

## Core Concepts

### 1. Stakeholder Types

**Internal:**
- Executive leadership
- Development team
- Sales and marketing
- Customer support
- Operations

**External:**
- Customers and users
- Partners and vendors
- Investors
- Regulators

### 2. Communication Channels

- Sprint reviews/demos
- Roadmap presentations
- Status reports
- Email updates
- One-on-one meetings
- Workshops
- Surveys and feedback forms

## Best Practices

### Communication
1. **Know your audience** - Tailor message and detail level
2. **Be concise** - Respect time, get to the point
3. **Use visuals** - Charts, demos, mockups
4. **Tell stories** - Make it relatable and memorable
5. **Be transparent** - Share progress and challenges
6. **Listen actively** - Understand concerns and feedback
7. **Follow up** - Document and share notes
8. **Regular cadence** - Consistent communication schedule

### Expectation Management
1. **Under-promise, over-deliver** - Build trust
2. **Communicate early** - Don't wait for problems
3. **Explain trade-offs** - Help understand priorities
4. **Provide context** - Share reasoning
5. **Set clear timelines** - Realistic commitments
6. **Acknowledge constraints** - Be honest about limitations
7. **Celebrate wins** - Recognize progress
8. **Address concerns** - Don't ignore issues
