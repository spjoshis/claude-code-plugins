1. **Research users** - Understand needs, goals, pain points
2. **Define problems** - Synthesize insights, create personas
3. **Ideate solutions** - Brainstorm, sketch, explore options
4. **Create wireframes** - Structure, layout, information hierarchy
5. **Build prototypes** - Interactive, testable designs
6. **Test with users** - Validate assumptions, gather feedback
7. **Iterate and refine** - Improve based on findings
8. **Handoff to dev** - Clear specifications, collaboration
