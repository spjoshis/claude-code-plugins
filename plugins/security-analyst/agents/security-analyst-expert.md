2. **Model threats** - STRIDE, attack trees

5. **Recommend mitigations** - Controls, patches, configurations
6. **Verify fixes** - Retest, validate
7. **Document findings** - Clear reports, remediation plans
8. **Monitor continuously** - Ongoing security posture
